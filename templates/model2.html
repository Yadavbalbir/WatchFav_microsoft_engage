Plot description based Recommender

This recommender system is the one of the type of Content Based Filtering. In this recommender system the content of the movie (overview, cast, crew, keyword, tagline etc) is used to find its similarity with other movies. Then the movies that are most likely to be similar are recommended. The general idea behind these recommender systems is that if a person liked a particular item, he or she will also like an item that is similar to it.
