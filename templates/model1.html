Demographic Filtering

Movie recommendations based on Demographic Filtering offer generalized recommendations to every user, based on movie popularity. The System recommends the same movies to users with similar demographic features. The basic idea behind this system is that movies that are more popular and critically acclaimed will have a higher probability of being liked by the average audience.
